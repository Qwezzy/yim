Our Three Core Pillars

At YIM we aim to become one of the leading service providers in Health and Welfare Development Programme, with acknowledged expertise in service provision at district, regional, provincial, and national level.

HEALTH

YIM Health development Centre is working at Ekurhuleni East, focusing on HIV, STI, TB and Human Rights. The organization started with volunteers providing Home-based care in 2007. From 2013 to 2016 other programmes like Social Mobilization, HTC, Linkage to Care, Condom Distribution, Orphan and Vulnerable Children, Sexual Reproductive Health on Adolescent and Youth, Stigma Reduction Programme in the Context of HIV, Gender Identity and Sexual Orientation, After-school Programme and Support on Prevention of Mother to Child Transmission were introduced.

SOCIAL DEVELOPMENT

  • Child Care Forum: Providing a safe space for children to receive care and support.
  • Psychosocial Support: Addressing the emotional and psychological needs of children.
  • Home Visits for Those on Treatment: Ensuring children receive necessary medical care.
  • Home Visits to Follow Academic Improvements: Monitoring and supporting children's educational progress.
  • Nutritional and Food Support: Providing essential nutrition to promote healthy development.

    EDUCATION

    Established in 2016, YIM Education Foundation has rapidly evolved into a leading non-governmental organization in South Africa, with a primary focus on afterschool programs. With branches strategically located in KwaZulu-Natal (KZN), Gauteng, and Mpumalanga, we are dedicated to unlocking the full potential of young minds, ensuring they receive the support and resources needed to excel academically and personally.
