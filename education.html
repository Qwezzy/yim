About YIM EDUCATION

Established in 2016, YIM Education Foundation has rapidly evolved into a leading non-governmental organization in South Africa, with a primary focus on afterschool programs. With branches strategically located in KwaZulu-Natal (KZN), Gauteng, and Mpumalanga, we are dedicated to unlocking the full potential of young minds, ensuring they receive the support and resources needed to excel academically and personally.

YIM EDUCATION

Since our inception, YIM Education Foundation has witnessed a steady growth in reach and impact. Our programs have positively influenced the lives of countless students, enabling them to achieve academic excellence, build character, and develop a sense of responsibility towards their communities.

YIM GARDENING

The project empowers vulnerable youth, men, and women to provide better access to healthy food and to promote sustainable social activities that could possibly reduce poverty and crime, for people to also acquire knowledge in landscaping, to create green gardens at a micro-regional level also promoting social habits for recycling and usage of waste in natural ways of composting and creating better living environments.

YIM SPORT.

Our Youth Sports Program is designed to empower young people, fostering personal growth, teamwork, and leadership skills through active participation in various sports activities. We are committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and encouraging environment where every child can thrive.
