HIV / AIDS

At Yakha Ikusasa Manje Health & Development Centre (YIM), our HIV/AIDS program is dedicated to combating the spread of HIV and supporting individuals affected by the virus. We strive to provide comprehensive services that promote health, awareness, and empowerment within our communities. We offer support groups for children and families affected by HIV/AIDS, providing emotional support, health education, and guidance on treatment adherence.

Sexually Transmitted Infection

We are committed to promoting sexual health awareness, providing confidential support, and ensuring access to testing and treatment services for all community members. Through education and compassionate care, we work to reduce stigma and empower individuals to take control of their sexual health. YIM is committed to creating a healthier future for all. By promoting awareness, providing essential services, and supporting those affected by HIV, we aim to reduce the impact of the epidemic in our communities.

Health and testing Services (HTS)

Our Health and Testing Services program provides comprehensive, confidential screening and support for sexual health. Our HTS team consists of certified healthcare professionals who provide non-judgmental, culturally sensitive care in a safe and supportive environment.

Condom Distribution

At Yakha Ikusasa Manje Health & Development Centre (YIM), we recognize that condom distribution is a vital component of our strategy to combat the spread of HIV and other sexually transmitted infections (STIs). Our comprehensive condom distribution program aims to promote safe sexual practices and empower individuals to take control of their sexual health.

Sexual Reproductive Health

At Yakha Ikusasa Manje Health & Development Centre (YIM), we are dedicated to promoting Sexual Reproductive Health (SRH) as a fundamental aspect of overall well-being. Our SRH program focuses on providing comprehensive education, resources, and services that empower individuals to make informed choices about their sexual and reproductive health.

Prevention of Mother to Child Transmission

Prevention of Mother-to-Child Transmission (PMTCT) is a critical initiative aimed at ensuring that mothers living with HIV can give birth to healthy, HIV-negative babies. At Yakha Ikusasa Manje Health & Development Centre (YIM), we are committed to providing comprehensive support and education to expectant mothers throughout their pregnancy and beyond.
