General Questions

By joining our team, you'll be part of a dedicated group of individuals working towards creating a better future for everyone. Get involved, make new friends, and help us achieve our mission. Sign up today and start making a difference!

We provides various services, including educational support through afterschool programmes, food security initiatives like community gardening, HIV/AIDS support groups, and psychosocial care for orphaned and vulnerable children (OVC). We also offer home-based care services for the elderly and chronically ill.

Our programmes are designed for children, youth, and vulnerable individuals in the community, including orphans, those affected by HIV/AIDS, and families facing food insecurity. We also provide employment and training opportunities for unemployed youth through tutoring and skills development.

We rely on a combination of donor funding, grants, corporate partnerships, and contributions from individuals. We also engage in fundraising initiatives to sustain our various programmes.

We have a monitoring and evaluation system in place to assess the effectiveness of our programmes. Regular feedback from participants, tutors, and community members helps us measure our impact and make improvements.

The After School Programme provides academic tutoring, life skills training, and extracurricular activities to help children improve their performance and personal development. It also offers psychosocial support to learners from vulnerable backgrounds. We actively seek partnerships with schools, local businesses, and other NGOs to expand our impact and reach more individuals in need.
